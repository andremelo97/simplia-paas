# Configurations

Configuration settings are split between the **TQ app** and the **Hub portal**.

## TQ Configurations (Admin Only)

TQ configurations are accessible from the **Configurations** menu in the TQ sidebar. Only users with the **Admin** license type can see this menu.

### Email Templates

- Navigate to **Configurations > Email Template**.
- Customize email templates used when sharing quotes and prevention documents with patients.
- There are **separate templates for quotes and prevention documents**.
- Configurable fields for each template:
  - Subject line
  - Body text
  - Greeting
  - CTA (Call to Action) button text
  - Footer

## Hub Configurations

Hub settings are accessible from the Hub portal at hub.livocare.ai.

### Branding

- Navigate to **Hub > Branding**.
- Configure your clinic's visual identity:
  - **Logo**: Upload your clinic logo (appears on documents and emails).
  - **Colors**: 3 configurable colors (primary, secondary, accent).
  - **Company Info**: Name, phone, email, address.
  - **Social Links**: Links to your social media profiles.
- Branding appears on printed documents and emails sent to patients.

### Communication (SMTP)

- Navigate to **Hub > Communication**.
- Configure SMTP settings for sending emails from your clinic.
- A default SMTP server is provided by LivoCare.
- You can customize with your own SMTP server for branded email delivery.

### Transcription Usage

- Navigate to **Hub > Transcription Usage**.
- View your current transcription minutes usage vs. your monthly quota.
- Default quota is 60 minutes per month.
- A warning appears at 80% usage; transcription is disabled at 100%.
- Quota resets monthly based on your billing cycle.
- Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Stripe Portal where you can:
  - Update your subscription plan
  - Change your payment method
  - View invoices

### Marketplace

- Navigate to **Hub > Marketplace**.
- Browse and manage available templates and landing pages.
- Import curated templates and landing page designs provided by LivoCare.
